About Me

Hi! My name is Tatum Harrison. I am a current student at the University of Florida, pursuing my Masters in Mass Communication with a concentration in Digital Strategy. I was born and raised in Tampa, Florida but now currently reside in Jacksonville, Florida where I completed my undergrad in English at the University of North Florida. At the moment, I work in digital content for a large retail and marketing agency that focuses primarily on CPG brands such as Coca-Cola, Smucker, Nestle, etc.

My Career Goals

While I have much experience in marketing, social media, and editing, I hope to utilize my experience in design, user experience, and marketing strategy to enter the into the field of UX design or product management.
